KEVIN CROMPWELL

I'm a 3D generalist, graphic designer, and lecturer from Upstate New York.

My current pursuits find me rendering 3D characters and scenes, designing interactive, real-time experiences, and lecturing part-time at the University of Rochester.

I have several years of experience working as an independent designer as well as in a studio. I'm committed to providing the highest caliber of quality design and have been known to go the extra mile to ensure that a design provides the power a client is seeking.

I have an affinity for keeping up with the most current technology and enjoy teaching or training others effective design methods and the tools behind it.

Some of my hobbies include: writing and dissecting Python scripts, coding websites (I wrote the vast majority of this site), noodling on my guitar, learning C++ for game development and interactive design, photogrammetry, and planning systems to help stay organized and effective.
